Boarding and Departure

  1. 1 Arrival Time:
    • Passengers should arrive at the boarding point at least 30 minutes before the scheduled departure time.
    • Early arrival ensures smooth boarding procedures and allows time for any unforeseen delays.
  2. 2 Ticket Verification:
    • Passengers must present their tickets (printed or electronic) along with valid identification at the boarding point.
    • The bus staff verifies the tickets and assigns seats to passengers based on their bookings.
  3. 3 Luggage Handling:
    • Passengers are responsible for their luggage.
    • They should ensure that their luggage is securely packed and labeled with their name and contact details.
    • Bulky or oversized luggage may be subject to additional fees and must comply with the bus company's baggage policy.
  4. 4 Boarding Process:
    • Passengers board the bus in an orderly manner, following the instructions of the bus staff.
    • The entire fare for the booked journey may be forfeited in case of no-show.
  5. 5 Seating Arrangements:
    • Passengers occupy their assigned seats as indicated on their tickets.
    • Seats are allocated based on the booking preferences and availability at the time of reservation.
  6. 6 Safety Protocols:
    • Passengers must adhere to safety protocols and guidelines specified by the bus company.
    • This may include wearing seat belts, refraining from smoking or consuming alcohol onboard, and following any emergency evacuation procedures.
  7. 7 Departure Time:
    • The bus departs at the scheduled departure time, regardless of whether all passengers have boarded.
    • Passengers who arrive after the departure time may forfeit their tickets and may need to book alternative transportation.
  8. 8 Communication Channels:
    • The bus company provides clear communication channels for passengers to inquire about boarding procedures, departure times, and any other concerns.
    • Contact information for customer support or emergency assistance is made available to passengers.
  9. 9 Onboard Amenities:
    • Passengers can enjoy onboard amenities such as comfortable seating, air conditioning/heating, Wi-Fi, and entertainment systems (if available).
    • They are expected to use these amenities responsibly and considerately towards fellow passengers.
  10. 10 Arrival at Destination:
    • Passengers disembark the bus upon arrival at their destination.
    • They collect their luggage and exit the bus terminal in an orderly manner.
