Cancellation Policy

  1. Cancellation charges:
    • Cancellation charges will be applicable based on the time of cancellation and the type of ticket purchased.
    • Cancellation charges may vary depending on factors such as the distance of the journey, type of bus, and fare class.
  2. Cancellation Timeframe:
    • Cancellation requests made before a certain period before the departure time will incur nominal cancellation charges.
    • Cancellation requests made within a specified period before the departure time will incur higher cancellation charges.
    • Cancellation requests made after a certain period before the departure time may not be eligible for a refund, and the entire fare may be forfeited.
  3. Refund Policy:
    • Refunds, if applicable, will be processed within a specified period from the date of cancellation.
    • Refunds will be credited to the original mode of payment used during ticket booking.
    • Processing fees and taxes, if any, may be deducted from the refund amount.
  4. No-Show Policy:
    • Passengers who do not show up for their scheduled departure ("no-show") without prior cancellation will not be eligible for any refund.
    • The entire fare for the booked journey may be forfeited in case of no-show.
  5. Change of Reservation:
    • Passengers may have the option to modify their reservation, subject to availability and applicable charges.
    • Changes to the reservation may incur additional fees, and the fare difference, if any, must be paid at the time of modification.
  6. Force Majeure Events:
    • In case of unforeseen circumstances such as natural disasters, strikes, or government regulations, the company reserves the right to cancel or reschedule services without prior notice.
    • In such situations, passengers may be entitled to a full refund or alternative arrangements, subject to the company's discretion.
  7. Special Circumstances:
    • Special cancellation policies may apply during peak seasons, festivals, or special events.
    • Passengers are advised to review the specific terms and conditions applicable to their booking for any deviations from the standard cancellation policy.
