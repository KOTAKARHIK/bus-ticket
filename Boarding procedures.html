Booking ProcedureFollow these simple steps to book your tickets

  1. 1 Visit the website or App:
    • Users visit the website or open the mobile app of GoBus!
  2. 2 Selection of Routes:
    • Users select their departure city, destination city, and date of travel to search for available routes.
  3. 3 Select Route:
    • From the search results, users select their preferred route.
  4. 4 Choose Seats:
    • Users are presented with available seats on the selected bus for the chosen route and date.
    • They can choose their preferred seats based on availability.
  5. 5 Enter Passenger Details:
    • Users enter details such as the number of passengers, their names, ages, and any special requirements.
    • They also provide contact information for communication and confirmation purposes.
  6. 6 Select Fare Type:
    • Users select the fare type based on their eligibility (regular, student, senior citizen, etc.).
  7. 7 Review and Confirm:
    • Users review their booking details, including route, date, seat selection, passenger information, and fare type.
    • They confirm the booking after ensuring all details are correct.
  8. 8 Make Payment:
    • Users proceed to the payment gateway to make payment for the booked tickets.
    • They choose their preferred payment method (credit/debit card, net banking, digital wallets, etc.).
  9. 9 Receive Confirmation:
    • Once the payment is successful, users receive a booking confirmation along with a booking reference number.
    • Confirmation details are sent via email and/or SMS to the provided contact information.
  10. 10 Retrieve Tickets:
    • Users can either print their tickets from the website/app or opt for e-tickets/mobile tickets.
    • E-tickets are sent to the registered email address and can be downloaded and displayed on mobile devices for boarding.
  11. 11 Boarding:
    • On the day of travel, users arrive at the boarding point well in advance.
    • They present their tickets (printed or electronic) along with valid identification to board the bus.
  12. 12 Travel, Feedback, and Support:
    • Users enjoy their journey, and GoBus! ensures a smooth travel experience.
    • After the journey, users may provide feedback on their experience through the website/app.
    • GoBus! offers customer support for any queries or assistance during and after the journey.
